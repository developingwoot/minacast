# Privacy Policy for Minacast

**Last updated: March 30, 2026**

## Overview

Minacast is a podcast player app for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Data stored locally on your device

Minacast stores the following data exclusively on your device:

- Podcast subscriptions you add
- Episode metadata (titles, descriptions, publication dates)
- Playback progress and completion status
- Downloaded audio files
- App settings (dark mode, playback speed, sleep timer)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

### Network requests made by the app

To function as a podcast player, the app makes the following outbound requests:

- **iTunes Search API** (`itunes.apple.com`) — to search for podcasts by name
- **Podcast RSS feeds** — to fetch episode listings for podcasts you subscribe to
- **Podcast audio URLs** — to stream or download episodes

These requests go directly from your device to the podcast provider or Apple's public API. Minacast does not operate any servers, does not proxy these requests, and does not log or store any data from them.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect any personally identifiable information
- We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries
- We do not create accounts or require sign-in
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not sell data

## Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app may send local notifications when new episodes are available. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent from any server.

## Data Deletion

All app data can be deleted by uninstalling the app. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
